About Biryani Bhai

Our Story

At Biryani Bhai, our journey began with a single spice, a vision of authenticity. From the bustling streets of India to your plate, our passion for biryani unites cultures, ignites palates, and creates memories.

Our Chef

Our head chef, Siddharth, has over 20 years of experience in the culinary industry. He has worked in some of the best Indian restaurants in the world and brings his expertise and passion to every dish he creates.
